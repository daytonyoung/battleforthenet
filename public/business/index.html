How we get Congress to save net neutrality.

This fall, while working to persuade members of Congress to oppose FCC chairman Ajit Pai, we found that the most effective form of persuasion—by far—was to organize pressure from local businesses. But it was a ton of work, and now we need to scale up from dozens of targets to hundreds. So we're moving our business outreach strategy to Twitter, and crowdsourcing it. By following the steps below, you can make sure every undecided member of Congress is getting pressure from local businesses.
